In June of 2022, the Colorado Avalanche celebrated their third Stanley Cup in the teams 22 year history. The Avalanche faced a wounded Nashville team in the first round, where they were able to close them out in 4 games. The next round the Avalanche faced the best opponent they would face coming from the Western Conference. The St. Louis Blues put up more of a fight, but they were still only able to steal 2 games from the Avs. The Avalanche's first Western Conference finals appearence in 20 year was against the Edmonton Oilers. The avalanche routed the Oil in 4 easy games, where Colorado out-scored Edmonton 22-13. Once in the Finals, the Avalanche faced Tampa Bay, the 2 time reigning champions. The Avalanhe and Lightning faced off in a series for the ages. Colorado was able to win in 6 games, leaving their entire playoff record at 16-4.
